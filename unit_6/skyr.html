Skyr

This campaign focused on the Nordic lifestyle and the benefits of their mindset, habits and healthy eating choices. This gave us an outlet to promote skyr yoghurt as a healthy ingredient within recipes. On the website we introduced five health and wellbeing experts, the user could select each person which then clicked through to their own recipe that used the yoghurt. My roll in this campaign was to design responsive webpages to sit within Stylist.co.uk and magazine page layouts for the recipe pages and editorial feature. I also organised and art directed the food photoshoot for this campaign.
