American Express

American express booked with Shortlist and Stylist two 32 page mini magazines. The idea behind the campaign was to highlight how many independent shops take American Express cards by producing two supplements about independent shop owners which were handed out with Shortlist and Stylist magazine. My role was to oversee and art direct the supplements and supervise a team. This involved, layout, shoot preparation, managing budgets, cover concept and flat planning.
