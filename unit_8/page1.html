Stella McCartney for Adidas

The campaign was to drive awareness of the Stella McCartney for Adidas range by focusing on three fitness influencers and how they tackle exercise in winter. The website contained video content on their daily routine and a scrolling section with their health tips. I oversaw this campaign from pitch stage. I designed responsive webpages and online ad’s to sit within Stylist.co.uk as well as designing the print campaign for the mag. I also organised the production of the video and art directed the video and stills shoot.
